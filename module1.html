Module Goal

To introduce participants to mindful awareness and compassion, creating a peaceful space within themselves from which to begin their healing journey.

Key Concepts

  • You Are a Safe Space: The first step isn't about fixing anything, but about realizing that peace and safety can be found within.
  • The Power of Observation: We will teach that you don't have to believe every thought or feel every emotion fully. You can simply notice it, like watching a cloud pass by. This is the foundation of freedom.
  • Small Acts of Kindness: Healing starts with tiny, consistent acts of kindness toward oneself.

The "Daily Blessings" Journaling Prompt

The Practice: Each morning or evening, find a quiet moment and a piece of paper or a notebook. Your only job is to write down **three things** you are grateful for from your day. This simple act is an expression of the Christ mindâ€”a recognition of the divine blessing in your life.

The Mindful Breath Meditation

The Practice: Find a comfortable position and gently close your eyes. Simply notice the rhythm of your breath without judgment. The breath is your anchor; it is always there, and you can always return to it. This practice creates a feeling of groundedness and inner peace.

The "Kindness Check-In" Practice

The Practice: Pause once a day and ask yourself, "What is one small, kind thing I can do for myself right now?" This could be drinking a glass of water, stretching, or simply giving yourself a moment of silence. This practice teaches you to intentionally choose kindness towards yourself.
